Alfredo Pasta

Fun Alfredo pasta Facts: Alfredo pasta was invented in Rome around 1908 by Alfredo di Lelio for his wife, Ines, to stimulate her appetite after childbirth. The original dish was a simple, creamy blend of butter, Parmesan cheese, and fettuccine, but the American version often includes heavy cream, multiple cheeses, and other ingredients like chicken or shrimp.
